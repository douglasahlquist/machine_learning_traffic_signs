Replace yield signs with stop signs in downtown Chico residentials

This petition had 1,244 supporters

dillon Mitchler started this petition to City of Chico

The residential area of downtown chico stretching from 2nd to 8th and salem to cherry is always very dangerous to drive/walk through. It is difficult to see oncoming cars and people tend to speed which makes driving through these intersections are very dangerous and sketchy. Drivers don't take the yield signs seriously and there are many times where they blow right through them. It's time to change all the yield signs downtown into stop signs so our community can be much safer.
